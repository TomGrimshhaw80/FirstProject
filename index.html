Arsenals summer 2022 transfer business analysed

Have the gunners improved their squad ahead of the new season?

After a disappointing end to the 2021-22 season, Arsenal have once again spent big money in an attempt to improve their squad, £120m as of August 4th 2022. Lets take a look at how they did...firstly with a look at who has come in:

  • Marquinhos - £3m
  • Matt Turner - £6m
  • Fabio Vieira - £30m
  • Gabriel Jesus - £45m
  • Oleksander Zinchenko - £30m

Marquinhos is an unknown quantity. An opportunistic purchase, the right sided attacked will likely go on loan

Matt Turner is the US national goalkeeper, but will be an adequate backup to Ramsdale

Fabio Vieira is statistically one of Europes best attackers, but will likely be a backup to the attacking players

Gabriel Jesus is a maquee, game changing signing, and likely to be up their with the PLs best players this season

Oleksander Zinchenko will make a huge difference in the build up phase, and add immense technical quality

In terms of MY PERSONAL thoughts on the relative quality of our signings:

  1. Gabriel Jesus
  2. Oleksander Zinchenko
  3. Fabio Vieira
  4. Matt Turner
  5. Marquinhos
